Hajime no Ippo

HAJIME NO IPPO is a series centered around Ippo Makunouchi (voiced in English by Steve Staley), a kind and timid high school student who's frequently beaten up by bullies. One day Ippo is rescued from his bullies by Mamoru Takamura (Eddie Frierson), a talented up-and-coming boxer.
