Code Geass

In the second season, Code Geass R2, Lelouch's identity as Zero is eventually revealed. He continues his fight against Britannia, now with the goal of destroying the current world order and recreating it as a more just and peaceful place. The story becomes more complex as Lelouch has to contend with other factions and individuals who have their own agendas. The series climaxes in an epic finale where Lelouch achieves his goal of overthrowing the Britannian Empire, at great personal cost.
