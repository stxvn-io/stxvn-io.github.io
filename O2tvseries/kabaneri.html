Kabaneri of the Iron Fortress

Kabaneri of the Iron Fortress is set during the Industrial Revolution of the Samurai age. Originally twenty years ago, a mysterious virus infected the inhabitants of Hinomoto Island turning them into the Kabane, aggressive reanimated creatures with near impenetrable metal heart cages.
